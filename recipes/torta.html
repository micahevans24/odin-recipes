Tortas Recipe

A delicious torta served with tortilla chips and dipping sauce.

This delicious sandwich was served on a warm bun, with onions and peppers.

It was accompanied by a bowl of savory dipping sauce, and crunchy tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Slow cook the beef.
  2. Carmelize the onions, peppers, and tomatoes in a pan.
  3. Shred the beef, layer in onions, peppers, and tomatoes, and stir together.
